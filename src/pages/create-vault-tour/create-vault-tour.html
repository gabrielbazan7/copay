Your funds, your bank
Vaults allow you to store your crypto funds directly on your computer or device. Similar to cash in physical wallet, only you can access control your money.
=
The “key” to your bank
Becoming your own bank means that you will need to protect your funds from theives. Each vault you create will have it’s own unique key that will need to kept privately and securely.
Becoming your own bank requires responsibility.
One last thing. Your vault will be managed securely on this device. So if ever your device is lost, stolen, or updated, your vault can only be restored with your vault key.
